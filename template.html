About Us

Shishu Model High School also known as SMHS. The school was established in 1984. Shishu Model High School is a Co-Ed school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) . It is managed by Shishu Model Educational Society.Our dedicated faculty are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive atmosphere where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. With a focus on personalized learning and innovative teaching methods, we empower our students to become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens.
